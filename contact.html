Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to common questions about our services and process.

What services do you offer?

We provide comprehensive architectural design, structural engineering, project management, and construction supervision services for residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Residential projects typically take 3-8 months, while commercial projects can range from 6-18 months from design to completion.

Do you handle permits and approvals?

Yes, we assist with all necessary permits, building code compliance, zoning approvals, and regulatory submissions to ensure your project meets all requirements.

What is your fee structure?

Our fees are project-based and depend on scope, complexity, and timeline. We provide detailed proposals after our initial consultation and project assessment.
